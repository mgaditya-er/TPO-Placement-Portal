About Us

Established in 2009, Government Residence Women Polytechnic Tasgaon is a premier institution dedicated to empowering women through technical education. The institute provides state-of-the-art facilities and a supportive learning environment, ensuring students are equipped with the necessary skills to excel in their careers.

Located in a serene environment, 3 km from Tasgaon city, the college spans over 22 acres and features academic buildings, residential quarters, and hostel zones. The curriculum is designed to develop technically proficient engineers with a strong ethical foundation.

Our vision is to foster innovation and excellence in technical education, empowering women to contribute meaningfully to society. Through various programs and hands-on training, we aim to bridge the gap between industry and academia.
